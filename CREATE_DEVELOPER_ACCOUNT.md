# How to Create Microsoft 365 Developer Account

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Go to Developer Program Website
1. Open your browser and go to: **https://developer.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/dev-program**
2. Click the **"Join now"** or **"Get started"** button

### Step 2: Sign In
1. You'll be asked to sign in
2. You can use your Gmail account - click **"Create account"** if you don't have a Microsoft account
3. If creating a new Microsoft account:
   - Use your Gmail address
   - Complete the verification process
   - Set a password

### Step 3: Complete Registration
1. Fill out the registration form:
   - Country/Region
   - Company name (can use your name or "Developer")
   - Accept terms and conditions
2. Click **"Next"** or **"Join"**

### Step 4: Get Your Developer Tenant
1. After registration, you'll be redirected to the developer dashboard
2. You'll receive an email with your tenant details
3. Your tenant URL will be something like:
   - `https://yourname.onmicrosoft.com`
   - Or a custom domain if you set one up

### Step 5: Access SharePoint
1. Go to: **https://www.office.com**
2. Sign in with your developer account
3. Click on **"SharePoint"** from the app launcher
4. You'll see your SharePoint sites

### Step 6: Access the Workbench
1. Navigate to any SharePoint site
2. Go to: `https://your-tenant.sharepoint.com/_layouts/workbench.aspx`
3. This is where you'll test your web parts!

## What You Get
- ✅ Free SharePoint Online tenant
- ✅ 25 user licenses (for testing)
- ✅ Valid for 90 days (renewable)
- ✅ Full SharePoint functionality
- ✅ Ability to create sites and lists
- ✅ Perfect for development and testing

## Quick Access Links
- **Developer Program:** https://developer.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/dev-program
- **Office 365 Portal:** https://www.office.com
- **SharePoint Admin:** https://admin.microsoft.com
